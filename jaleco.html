Jaleco App

Schedule and Medical Management for Doctors and Patients.

The Jaleco platform allows doctors to access their appointment schedule and medical records on their laptop, tablet or smartphone. Patients enjoy the convenience of scheduling an appointment and monitoring their treatment in the comfort of their own home, while still having access to the care team when they need it. It's the best of both worlds.




About Project

In this project I am responsible for all the Front-End development of the Web App. It was developed in Angular 8 and has two main modules, one for clinics to register doctors and manage consultations, and the other for doctors with independent offices to manage their agenda.
